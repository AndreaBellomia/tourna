# Contributing to Tourna

Thank you for your interest in Tourna.

## Before Contributing

This project does not accept uncoordinated contributions.

If you want to collaborate on the application, you must contact the author first to discuss:

- the scope of the contribution
- the architectural direction
- the expected coding standards
- ownership and review flow

## Contact

- Author: Andrea Bellomia
- Email: andreabellomia2001@gmail.com

## Contribution Policy

- Open an issue or write to the author before starting substantial work.
- Do not open large pull requests without prior alignment.
- Contributions may be declined if they do not fit the project direction or quality bar.
- By proposing changes, you agree to follow the repository instructions in `.codex/` and `AGENTS.md`.

## Engineering Expectations

- Respect package boundaries and architectural ownership.
- Prefer maintainable solutions over quick fixes.
- Keep contracts, domain logic, persistence, and transport concerns clearly separated.
- Run `pnpm lint`, `pnpm check-types`, and `pnpm test` before proposing changes.

## Commercial Exceptions

The repository license does not allow commercial use by default.

Any exception for commercial use, resale, paid hosting, paid support bundles, or derivative commercial offerings requires explicit written authorization from the author.
