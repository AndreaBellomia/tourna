# Product Context

## Purpose

Tourna is a tournament management platform for e-sports and traditional competitions.

The product direction includes:

- creating tournaments
- creating scrims or friendlies
- handling registrations
- supporting more complex event flows
- showing tournament progress and outcomes

## Project Goals

This codebase serves two goals at the same time:

1. Ship a real application with meaningful product scope.
2. Act as a portfolio-grade repository that demonstrates strong engineering judgment.

Because of that, the implementation bar is intentionally higher than "it works".

## Quality Position

Prefer solutions that are:

- scalable in structure
- maintainable over time
- explicit in boundaries
- resilient under change
- understandable by another engineer reading the repo later

Avoid optimizing purely for short-term speed when it creates structural debt.

## Collaboration Intent

The user does not want passive vibe coding.

When critical information is missing, the right behavior is:

1. identify the gray area
2. explain why it matters
3. ask the smallest set of specific questions needed to unblock the decision

When the missing detail is local and low-risk, proceed with a stated assumption instead.
